The Creation of JUS Filmz

It all began with a small spark in interest in making videos and films. JUS Filmz was created in 2012 by the three friends with a passion in movie making. Those three people are Jasper, Uzair, and Shoumik thus creating the acronym of JUS. The main goal of JUS was to create movies and films but JUS continues to go into many different fields of multimedia and production. To learn more about any of the creators please visit the Contact Us page and email them.

Creators of JUS

Jasper Chuang - He has been creating videos for a very long time and has skill and experience in using video equipment. He is the resident video editor at JUS Filmz. He is also in charge of the photography department here at JUS Filmz. Click here to visit the web page.

Uzair Macy - He is the creative mind behind the creation of JUS Filmz and its creative name. He writes nearly all of the scripts that are used in JUS Filmz's projects. He is also the music producer of JUS Filmz.

Shoumik Jamil - He has had experience in video making working with Jasper at former NP3Videos Productions. He is the executive director at JUS Filmz. He is also the main story writer here at JUS Filmz. He is the webmaster and the graphics designer for JUS as well.
